Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our MPAP programs

MPAP is designed for students from primary through junior high school (ages 6-15). We offer age-appropriate programs tailored to different learning levels and mathematical abilities.

Our programs run in 12-week cycles with sessions held twice per week. Each session is 90 minutes long, providing comprehensive coverage of mathematical concepts while maintaining student engagement.

All students receive comprehensive workbooks, practice materials, and access to our digital learning platform. Additional resources include video tutorials, interactive exercises, and progress tracking tools.

Enrollment is simple! Contact us through our website or visit our office. We'll schedule an initial assessment to determine the best program for your child and guide you through the registration process.

We maintain small class sizes of 12-15 students to ensure personalized attention and effective learning. Our remedial program has even smaller groups of 6-8 students for intensive support.

We use regular assessments, continuous evaluation, and our digital platform to track each student's progress. Parents receive monthly progress reports and have access to real-time performance data through our parent portal.
