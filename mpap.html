MPAP Program Overview

The Challenge: Why Maths Outcomes Need Attention


In Ghana’s evolving educational landscape, mathematics remains one of the most important foundational skills. It supports success in science, technology, and everyday problem-solving. Yet many learners continue to fall short of the Minimum Proficiency Standards set by NaCCA. In many schools, assessment focuses mainly on final scores. While these scores show performance levels, they do not explain why students are struggling. This leaves teachers without clear direction, leading to generalized interventions and slow progress.

The MPAP Solution: From Scores to Insight


The Math Proficiency Acceleration Program (MPAP), developed by Scribble Works, addresses this challenge by shifting the focus from testing to understanding. MPAP provides clear insights into specific learning gaps, whether in decimals, algebra, or geometry. Instead of guessing, educators receive actionable data that helps them intervene exactly where it matters most. At its core, MPAP is built on a simple idea: when teachers understand student challenges clearly, they can teach more effectively.

MPAP follows a structured and practical four-step process:
  • Diagnostic Assessment to identify learning gaps
  • Actionable Reporting to highlight areas of difficulty
  • Targeted Reinforcement to address specific needs
  • Impact Measurement to track improvement
This cycle transforms everyday classroom activity into a continuous process of learning and progress .

During the reinforcement phase, identified gaps are addressed through a blended approach:
  • Structured practice using Assignment Maths Workbooks
  • Step-by-step reinforcement via the AssignmentX platform
  • Hands-on learning through Maths Practical Bootcamps
This ensures that learning is not only identified but effectively improved through meaningful and engaging experiences .

The results of MPAP are measurable and compelling. In one implementation, average scores improved from 46.8% to 54.4%, and the number of high-performing students increased significantly. In another, learners showed strong improvement in topics such as Linear Equations and the Pythagorean Theorem . These outcomes show that when teaching is guided by accurate data and targeted support, improvement can be accelerated.

MPAP is designed for flexibility and scale:
  • Schools – Improve performance and provide clear progress data to parents
  • Education Offices – Monitor learning outcomes across multiple schools
  • NGOs and Donors – Implement programs with measurable impact and transparency

What sets MPAP apart is its focus on understanding, not just testing. It moves learning away from memorization and toward real comprehension. It replaces guesswork with evidence and ensures that every learner has a clear pathway toward proficiency .

Improving mathematics education requires more than assessment, it requires insight, action, and continuous support. MPAP represents a shift toward clarity, accountability, and meaningful learning outcomes. It is not just a program, but a movement to transform how mathematics is taught and learned across Ghana and beyond .
